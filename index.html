Game Overview

GhostHunter is a retro, halloween themed action maze chase game. The game follows the main character who wakes suddenly in a maze-like graveyard that is guarded by the Grim Reaper and his vengeful ghosts. In order to escape, the main character must save all the ghosts stuck in the graveyard before the evil ghosts catch him.

Controls

Use up, down, left, and right arrow keys to move player around

Press M key to mute music
